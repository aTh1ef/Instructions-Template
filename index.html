Instructions:

  • Please use a PC; it's recommended⚠️
  • Perform the pose displayed on the screen correctly
  • If the pose detected by the camera is correct, a countdown timer from 10 to 0 starts.
  • If the countdown doesn't start, adjust your pose according to the displayed image on the screen and then the countdown should begin eventually.
  • Incorrect poses reset the timer back to 10 seconds
  • There are 6 levels in total, each with different poses.
  • Complete all poses for better yoga practice.
